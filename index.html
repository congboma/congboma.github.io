About

I am currently a post-doctoral associate at New York University Abu Dhabi, working with Dr. Farah Emad Shamout. Before that, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at Macquarie University, where I worked with Prof. Jia Wu and Prof. Jian Yang. During this period, I was also a visiting researcher at MBZUAI, working with Prof. Preslav Nakov.

I received my PhD from the University of Adelaide, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Wei (Emma) Zhang, Dr. Mingyu Guo, and Dr. Weitong Chen. Prior to my doctoral studies, I gained research experience as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Steven Hoi at Singapore Management University.

My research interests include data mining, medical machine learning, and multimodal learning.
