Congbo Ma

Postdoctoral Research Fellow
School of Computing
Macquarie University






I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Macquarie University, working with Associate Professor Jia Wu. During my doctoral studies, I worked under the supervision of Dr. Wei (Emma) Zhang, Dr. Mingyu Guo and Dr. Weitong Chen at the University of Adelaide. Prior to this, I gained research experience as a research assistant while working under the supervision of Professor Steven Hoi at the Singapore Management University. My research interests lie in natural language processing, data mining, medical machine learning, and multi-modal learning, etc.
